On March 1, I handed a Claude-based paralegal agent my entire active matter queue — 64 matters across contract, employment, and dispute resolution. I gave it read access to the document management system, calendar integration, and explicit instructions to surface anything urgent.

What followed was, on balance, the most productive thirty days of my practice in five years. It was also the closest I came to malpractice in fifteen.
