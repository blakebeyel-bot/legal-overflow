The associates I spoke with for this piece entered law school in 2022 and graduated in 2025. They are the first cohort who had access to generative AI the entire time they were being trained as lawyers.

None of them can BlueBook from memory.

All of them can ship a first draft that would have taken their 2018-cohort predecessors a full day of careful work. Whether that is a gain or a loss depends on what you think law practice is *for*.
